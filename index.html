Jan 24 - Jul 24

Development of Indicator and Power Latching Circuits | Research Intern, Mobile Robotics Lab, IIT Kanpur
Mentor: Prof. Shakti S. Gupta (Department of Mechanical Engineering, IIT Kanpur)
Objective
  • Design a PCB to indicate the voltage level of a 10,000 mAh battery used in a novel 4 legged robot SVAN M2
  • Design a PCB to regulate power supply in SVAN M2 with the help of a toggle and an emergency stop switch
Approach
  • Utilized a LM324ADR Integrated Circuit to control illumination of 4 LEDs, to display dynamic voltage of battery
  • Integrated a momentary latch switch to control power, allowing the circuit to turn on and off with a single press
  • Used 4 high power connectors on the latching circuit board to supply current upto 200 amperes to the robot
  • Utilised 10 MOSFETs in parallel and 8 cm thick copper traces for thermal management of power latching circuit
Result
  • The indicator circuit activates between 25V to 19V, powering itself on when the momentary switch is toggled
